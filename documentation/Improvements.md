**Ndotoni Growth Plan**

**Where We Are**

Ndotoni has a working platform and is already attracting visitors.  

However, our biggest challenge is growing the number of landlords, agents, and listings on the platform.

Without enough listings:  
• Renters have fewer options.  
• Traffic does not convert effectively.  
• Growth becomes difficult.

Our primary focus is acquiring landlords and agents.

**Our Main Goal**

Build a repeatable system that helps us continuously acquire:  
• Landlords  
• Agents  
• Property managers  
• Listings  

Everything we do should support this goal.

**Why Landlord Acquisition Matters**

Every landlord we onboard increases the number of listings available on Ndotoni and creates future revenue opportunities.  

To encourage growth, we are willing to invest in referral rewards during the early stages of the company.

**How We Plan To Do It**

**1. Direct Outreach**  
We will actively contact landlords, agents, and property managers through Facebook, WhatsApp, referrals, and personal networks.

**2. Student Ambassador Program**  
Students often know their landlords, property managers, and housing agents.  
We will recruit student ambassadors who can introduce landlords to Ndotoni and help spread awareness of the platform.

**3. Referral Program**  
We want people to help us find landlords.

**Reward Structure:**  
• TZS 2,000 when a referred landlord publishes their first listing.  
• 10% of Ndotoni’s fee when that landlord successfully rents through Ndotoni.

**Example:**  
If Ndotoni earns TZS 100,000 from a rental, the referrer earns TZS 10,000.

**Protecting Against Fraud**  
To qualify:  
• The landlord must be new to Ndotoni.  
• The landlord must have a unique phone number.  
• The landlord must provide identification.  
• Ndotoni may verify information and conduct physical verification when necessary.

During the pilot phase, each participant can earn rewards for up to 5 landlords.

**What Success Looks Like**

• More landlords  
• More agents  
• More listings  
• More successful rentals
